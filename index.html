ABOUT US

Malhar is the cultural fest of UIT RGPV , Bhopal. It hosts three events namely vocal mizzle, literary mizzle and mental mizzle.These events are organized in collaboration with IO News, Yours Creatively, Ragini and Level up Gamez . Vocal mizzle includes a singing competition for about 2:50 min given to each participant. Literary mizzle is n open mic platform for participants to showcase any talent they want.and mental mizzle includes a chess competition where you need to watch your moves and most importantly your opponents moves.

Events

Chess

It is a chess competition which will be organized on an online platform of chess.com. Depending on the no. Of participants the mode of game will be decided whether it is leaque or a knockout round.

Singing

Vocal mizzle is a singing competition where participants are free choose any song they want hindi Or english. Participants will be given a max time limit of 2.59 mins to give their performance

Open Mic

It is an open mic platform where participants are free to perform anything they are comfortable with be it standup comedy storytelling or something else. But the decency should be kept in mind.
